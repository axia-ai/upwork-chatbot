# Shipping & Delivery

North Star Outfitters offers two shipping speeds:

- **Standard shipping:** 3–5 business days.
- **Expedited shipping:** 1–2 business days.

## Details
- Shipping speed is chosen at checkout.
- Standard delivery typically arrives within 3 to 5 business days.
- Expedited delivery typically arrives within 1 to 2 business days.
- To check where a specific order is, ask the assistant to track your order and
  provide your order number.

## Order tracking
For the status of a specific order, the assistant can look it up by order number.
Have your order number ready (for example, #111, #222, or #333).
