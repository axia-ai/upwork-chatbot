# Frequently Asked Questions

## About North Star Outfitters
North Star Outfitters is an outdoor-apparel and camping-gear store. The support
assistant can help track orders, explain returns and exchanges, recommend gear,
and connect you with a human agent.

## Speaking with a person
You can ask to speak with a live agent at any time. When you do, the conversation
becomes a support ticket marked **In Progress** and a teammate follows up.

## What the assistant can help with
- **Order tracking** — provide your order number for the latest status.
- **Returns & exchanges** — 30-day policy on unused items in original packaging.
- **Product recommendations** — answer a couple of quick questions and get a
  category and product suggestion.
- **Human handoff** — ask for a person and we'll connect you with a live agent.
